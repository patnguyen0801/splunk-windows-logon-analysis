## Key Observations

- EventCode 4624 indicates successful logons.
- EventCode 4672 shows privileged access (SYSTEM activity).
- EventCode 4634 confirms session termination.
- EventCode 4648 indicates explicit credential usage.

## Security Considerations

- Privileged logons (4672) should be monitored.
- Excessive 4624 activity may indicate abnormal behavior.
- 4648 events can indicate credential misuse.
